## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no catch. The audit is free forever. 
   Credex makes money if you choose to buy discounted credits — 
   but that's entirely optional and only surfaced when relevant.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine is sourced from official vendor pricing 
   pages and verified weekly. We cite every source in our methodology.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Your tool selections and spend numbers are stored to generate your 
   shareable URL. Your email is only collected if you choose to share it. 
   We never sell data.

**Q: What tools do you cover?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, Anthropic API, ChatGPT, OpenAI API, 
   Gemini, and Windsurf — covering the majority of AI spend for 
   engineering and knowledge work teams.

**Q: What happens after I submit my email?**
A: You get one email with your audit summary. If your savings exceed $500/mo, 
   a Credex team member may reach out about discounted credits. 
   You can opt out anytime.
