# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Based on publicly available 
information about AI credit markets, a typical deal involves:

- **Average credit purchase:** $5,000–$25,000 (estimate; range reflects SMB 
  to mid-market buyers)
- **Credex margin:** ~15–25% on sourced credits (estimated)
- **Revenue per deal:** $750–$6,250
- **Blended estimate used below:** $2,000 revenue per closed deal

This is conservative. Enterprise deals likely skew higher.

## Conversion Funnel

Landing page visit        100%
Audit completed            28%   (industry avg for free tools: 25–35%)
Email captured             22%   (of completers — value shown first)
High-savings flagged        8%   (>$500/mo savings — Credex CTA shown)
Consultation booked         3%   (of total audits)
Credit purchase closed      1%   (of total audits)

At 1,000 audits/month:
- 30 consultations booked
- 10 deals closed
- $20,000 revenue/month at $2,000/deal blended

## What Makes This Profitable at Scale

The tool's marginal cost per audit is near zero:
- Anthropic API call: ~$0.01–0.05 per summary
- Supabase storage: negligible
- Vercel hosting: free tier covers early scale

Break-even: 1 closed deal covers ~$200–500/mo in hosting + API costs.
The tool is profitable from deal #1.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**Month 1–3:** 500 audits/mo, 5 deals/mo → $10k MRR
**Month 4–6:** 2,000 audits/mo via word of mouth + Credex customer base, 
               20 deals/mo → $40k MRR
**Month 7–12:** 5,000 audits/mo with embeddable widget + referral loop, 
                50 deals/mo → $100k MRR
**Month 13–18:** Enterprise motion kicks in — larger deals, $10k+ average, 
                 83 deals/mo needed → $1M ARR

**What must be true:**
1. Audit-to-consultation conversion holds at 3%
2. Credex closes 33% of consultations
3. Average deal size grows to $3,000+ by month 12
4. The tool maintains organic distribution (Show HN effect sustains via 
   word of mouth and result sharing)

These are achievable but not guaranteed. The biggest risk is deal size — 
if average revenue per deal stays at $750, the required deal volume 
triples and the timeline extends to 30+ months.
